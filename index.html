About Our Movement

Flag Force Plymouth is a grassroots movement of British patriots who believe in celebrating our national identity through the proud display of our flags. We are ordinary people from across Plymouth who refuse to be ashamed of our British heritage.

Starting in Plymouth and expanding across Devon and Cornwall, our volunteer-led organization coordinates flag displays and works to ensure that British patriotism remains visible and celebrated in our communities.

Our movement represents unity, pride, and the fundamental belief that flying the St George's Cross and Union Flag is something to be celebrated, not hidden. We welcome all patriots who share our love of Britain.
