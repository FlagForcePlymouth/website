Questions & Answers

What is this all about?

We fly the St George's and Union flags to celebrate pride in our country, culture, and values — nothing more, nothing less. Our group was formed after seeing councils removing flags and people mislabelling them. We believe it's wrong to tie our national flags to politics.

Despite what you may have heard online, we welcome diversity and encourage everyone who loves our nation to join the movement, regardless of race, religion, gender, or orientation.

How can I support you?

Please follow and share our social media, and consider donating to our GoFundMe. All funds go toward flags for the Plymouth community.

Visit our GoFundMe page

Can you flag my street?

Rest assured we intend to cover all areas of Plymouth in due course. We receive over 100 individual requests daily and cannot prioritise individual areas. Sorry.

Where can I buy flags?

We can't give any recommendations, unfortunately. Our flags come from wholesalers with high minimum orders, which aren't practical for most people.

Do you have an email?

Yes, but for press enquiries only. Other queries will be deleted. Please see our Facebook bio for the current address.
